# 📊 Excel Sales Data Analysis  

## 🔹 Project Overview  
This project was completed as part of a **Data Technician Bootcamp** and focuses on analyzing **retail and sales data** using **Microsoft Excel**.  
The aim was to demonstrate my ability to **clean, analyze, and visualize sales data** using formulas, PivotTables, and charts to create meaningful insights that support decision-making.  

---

## 🔹 Dataset Description  
The dataset contains sales transactions with the following fields:  

- 🆔 **Transaction ID** – Unique identifier for each sales transaction  
- 📅 **Day / Month / Year / Date** – Transaction date details  
- 🙍 **Customer ID** – Unique identifier for customers  
- 🚻 **Gender** – Customer gender  
- 🎂 **Age** – Age of customer  
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Generation** – Customer generation group (e.g., Gen Z, Millennials)  
- 🛒 **Product Category** – Category of product sold  
- 🔢 **Quantity** – Number of units sold  
- 💲 **Price per Unit** – Selling price of each unit  
- 💰 **Total Sales** – Total sales value (Quantity × Price per Unit)  
- 🏦 **Commission 2023** – Commission earned in 2023  
- 🏦 **Commission 2024** – Commission earned in 2024  

---

## 🔹 Key Skills & Techniques  

- **Formulas & Functions**  
  - Used **SUM, SUMIF, AVERAGE, AVERAGEIF** for aggregations.  
  - Extracted date components using **DATE, MONTH, YEAR** functions.  
  - Applied **UNIQUE** to identify distinct values.  
  - Implemented **VLOOKUP** for lookup operations.  
  - Leveraged the **SWITCH** function for conditional categorization.  

- **Data Analysis**  
  - Filtered and sorted sales data for targeted exploration.  
  - Built **PivotTables** to summarize sales by product category, gender, generation, and time.  
  - Created dynamic reports for commissions across multiple years.  

- **Visualization**  
  - Designed charts (bar charts, line charts, and pie charts) to display:  
    - Sales trends over time  
    - Top-selling product categories  
    - Sales by gender and generation  
    - Yearly commission comparisons  

---

## 🔹 Business Insights  
- Identified **top-performing product categories** and their contribution to revenue.  
- Highlighted **customer trends** by age group and generation.  
- Showed **seasonal sales patterns** across different months and years.  
- Compared **commissions between 2023 and 2024** to evaluate growth.  

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies  
- **Microsoft Excel** (Formulas, PivotTables, PivotCharts, Filters, Sorting, Conditional Functions)
